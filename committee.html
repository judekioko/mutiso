Message from the Manager

I am delighted to welcome our readers to our edition of the **Constituency Development Fund (CDF) Newsletter**, which offers a variety of news on activities across our constituency.

The **CDF responsibility continues to grow**, as demonstrated by budgetary allocations. This has necessitated enforcement of efficiency measures by the Masinga NG-CDF committee. As the **manager**, I urge the people of Masinga to recognize that **CDF is a people-driven program**, where projects must be vetted through a consultative process guided by local leadership.

The committee is further authorized to **establish the viability of projects** from relevant government officers before implementation. I assure the people of Masinga that I am devoted to serving them diligently and ensuring that the **funds meant for development** are strictly used for their intended purpose.

Additionally, we will **work closely with the committee** to seek expert advice from both **government and private sources**, ensuring the highest level of **efficiency and accountability**.
